Ai-On-The-Edge-Cam is an ESP32-S3 board with Ethernet, PoE, SD card, camera, and a Stemma QT connector, compatible with the [Ai-On-The-Edge-Device firmware](https://github.com/allexoK/AI-on-the-edge-device/tree/esp32s3-test) for analog display reading at the edge and [Circuitpython](https://github.com/adafruit/circuitpython).
