Bootloader for One ROM, the most flexible replacement ROM for your retro system.

One ROM emulates a wide range of mask programmed ROMs, EPROMs and EEPROMs, including 24, 28 and 32 pin 8-bit ROMs, and 40 pin 16-bit ROMs.  It can be used to replace the original ROMs used in thousands of types of microprocessor based systems from the late 1970s, 1980s and 1990s.

One ROM is based around Raspberry Pi's RP2350 microcontroller and uses custom, bare metal firmware.
